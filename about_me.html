Hello! I'm Ruijia Hua

Beyond my technical work, I have contributed to machine learning research, with a publication on GAN-based image inpainting, and have built various projects, including a gesture-controlled YouTube player and a smart curtain system.

I'm always eager to explore new technologies and solve real-world challenges through software engineering and data science.
